manlapud egipto linmay abram ed bandad abalaten na canaan a kaiba to si sarai ya asawa to tan amin a kayaryan to akilad sikato si lot mayamyaman lay abram ta amayamay la ray karniro kanding baka pilak tan balituk to tinmaynan lamet diman tan nanalisalis so nankampoan to ya anggad akasabid betel insan asabi to may pasen a nanleetan na betel tan ai a nanayaman to nen saman tan nanggawaan toy altar diman to inggalang so katawan si lot a kaiba nen abram et wala met iray karniro to kanding tan saray baka to wala met so dilin pamilya to tan saray dilin aripen to kulang so pampastolan no manolup iran dua ta dakerakel iray ayayep da lapud saya mabetbet so siblangan da ray pumapastol nen abram tan saray pumapastol nen lot diad saman a panaon manaayam nid man iray cananeo tan saray perezeo inkuanen abram ed kinen lot sankanayon ita tan ag nepeg a mansebeg iray totook tan saray totoom say maung mansiyan ita la pilim so labalabay mon dalin lakad sakey a pasen onla ak balet ed sananey ninengneng sirin nen lot so kaliberliber tan abantag ton say patar na jordan ya anggad zoar et dakel so danum to a singa tanamanan na katawan o singa dalin na egipto diad saman a panaon ag ni dineral na katawan so siudad na sodoma tan say siudad na gomorra kanyan pinili nen lot a betang to so intiron patar na jordan insan amaarap diman ed bokig onyay impansian dan dua tinmilak si abram ed canaan balet nanayam si lot ed saray siudad diad patar ya asingger ed sodoma maoges iray totood man mapalaloy kasalanan dad katawan kasiput nen lot inkuan na katawan ed kinen abram manlapud tan ed kawalaan mo nengneng mon maung so kaliberliber mo amin a dalin a nabantag mo iter kod sika tan diad saray kabyangay boleg mo tan kayaryan mo ra ya anggad angga diad karakel na iter kod sikan kabyangay boleg mo no bilangen mo ra singa ka mambibilang na dabuk natan sirin liber mo yan dalin ta iter kod sikan amin inyalis nen abram so tulda to ed abay na saray masanton kiew ed mamre diad hebron tan nanayam
